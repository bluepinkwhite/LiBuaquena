Labels :)
Especially when it comes to the community and society in general, there's a lot of pressure to come up with a label or a box to put yourself in. "Gay", "Lesbian", "Bisexual", etc. But as a young queer person, I know personally how hard it is to choose one label that will make people assume things for you for the rest of your life. Remember that there is nothing wrong with just identifying as "queer" or "questioning" <3.


Lesbian!!
Simply put, a Lesbian is someone who is a non-man who exclusively
likes other non-men. Non-binary people and other genders who
don't necessarily identify as women (except for men) can be lesbians.
