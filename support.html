ways to support




Education is just the beginning. Here are more ways to support the LGBTQ+ community.

  1. Use inclusive language. When referring to people in general, use gender-neutral terms. Don't assume a person's gender or pronouns. Use they/them if you don't know, or ask the person if you can.
  2. Queer people don't owe you their identity. Whether or not someone decides to come out to you (tell you about their identity), respect their decision.

Links to LGBTQ+ organizations:

  1. The Trevor Project is a suicide prevention and crisis intervention organization for LGBTQ+ youth.
  2. GLAAD is a media monitoring organization that shares stories from the LGBTQ+ community to promote acceptance.
